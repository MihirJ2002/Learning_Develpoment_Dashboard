
## **Project: Strategic Learning Intelligence: L&D Analytics Dashboard**

## **Industry Type: Human Resources | Learning & Development | Workforce Analytics**

---

## **Business Scenario:**

The organization is investing heavily in employee Learning & Development programs to improve workforce skills, productivity, engagement, and departmental performance.

Training programs such as Advanced Excel, Data Analytics, AI Basics, Leadership Skills, Communication Skills, Project Management, and Time Management are delivered through Online, Offline, and Hybrid modes across multiple departments.

However, management currently lacks a centralized analytical system to measure training effectiveness, ROI, completion rates, learner engagement, instructor performance, and department-wise skill improvement.

To solve this, the organization requires an interactive L&D Analytics Dashboard that acts as a single source of truth for training performance, budget utilization, and workforce development insights.

---

## **The "Pain Points":**

The system must eliminate:

1. Low visibility into training effectiveness

2. Low completion rates in selected training programs

3. Negative or low ROI from high-cost programs

4. Uneven performance improvement across departments

5. Poor engagement and feedback in some training sessions

6. Difficulty in identifying best-performing programs, instructors, and delivery modes

7. Inefficient training budget allocation

8. Lack of data-driven decision-making in workforce development

---

## **The "Analyst's Mission":**

Design an interactive L&D Analytics Dashboard that:

1. **Tracks Training Performance**

* Monitor completion rate, training hours, feedback score, and engagement score

* Compare training performance across departments, roles, and programs

2. **Improves ROI Management**

* Identify high ROI and low ROI training programs

* Optimize training investments based on business impact

3. **Enhances Employee Performance**

* Measure pre-training and post-training performance improvement

* Track department-wise and role-wise performance gain

4. **Improves Learning Engagement**

* Analyze learner engagement and feedback scores

* Identify programs with low satisfaction or poor participation

5. **Optimizes Delivery Methods**

* Compare Online, Offline, and Hybrid training effectiveness

* Recommend the best delivery mode for each program type

6. **Supports Budget Optimization**

* Analyze program cost against ROI and performance gain

* Reduce spending on low-impact training programs

7. **Enables RCA & SWOT Analysis**

* Identify root causes of low ROI and poor completion

* Build SWOT-based strategies for L&D improvement
